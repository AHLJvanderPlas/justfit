The Engine

7 rules that cover
real life.

Every plan runs through the same transparent ruleset. No black box.

No time
→ Switches to micro session (≤10 min)
Poor sleep
→ Intensity capped at low
Low energy
→ Volume reduced by 40%
High stress
→ Shifts to mobility / recovery
Pain reported
→ Rest session, no loading
No clothing change
→ Stealth mode (standing, quiet)
Traveling / no gear
→ Bodyweight only
