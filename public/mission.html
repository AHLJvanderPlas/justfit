Mission, Vision & Philosophy

Version 1.0 — Effective 1 April 2025

Scope

This document describes the founding principles behind JustFit — why we built it, what we believe in, and the philosophy that guides every product decision.

Our Vision

Make adaptive, trustworthy personal coaching available every day — without requiring perfect motivation, perfect health, or perfect circumstances.

Our Mission

Help people stay consistently active by turning real-life constraints into safe, clear, daily training decisions.

Sleep bad? Stressed? Travelling? Injured? JustFit adjusts so you still move — safely, appropriately, and without skipping days.

Product Principles

These seven principles are our decision framework. When a feature or rule is unclear, the right answer is the one most consistent with these principles.

1. Consistency beats intensity. A completed session is better than a perfect session that gets skipped. We reward showing up, not suffering through.

2. Real life outranks plan purity. Sleep, stress, pain, travel, schedule, pregnancy, postnatal state, and recovery signals may immediately change the session. The plan serves you — not the other way around.

3. Safety beats ambition. The system must prefer safe downgrades over risky progression. When in doubt, the conservative choice is always the right choice.

4. The planner must be explainable. Meaningful plan changes are traceable to understandable rules. No black-box decisions. No hallucinated workouts.

5. One active training intent at a time. The engine considers many signals, but it always knows the single primary thing it is optimising for today.

6. Privacy is part of the product. Data minimisation, explicit consent, clear storage boundaries, and user control are core product behaviour — not just legal text.

7. During training, speed wins. Workout interactions are designed to be one-thumb, glanceable, and usable in about 2 seconds.

What JustFit Is — and Is Not

JustFit is: a privacy-first adaptive daily training coach, a deterministic rule-based planner, a practical execution coach during workouts, and a consistency tool.

JustFit is not: a social fitness network, a medical device or medical advisor, a black-box AI coach, a "max performance at any cost" app, or a feature-heavy platform where every mode competes equally.

Why We Don't Use AI for Plans

Your plan is generated by a transparent, rule-based engine — not a black box. Every decision (intensity, exercise selection, rest) is traceable to a specific rule. You can trust it, audit it, and understand it.

Privacy First

Your fitness data is yours. We separate your identity from your health data at the database level. We do not sell data and do not profile you for ads. We use a minimal set of service providers (infrastructure and transactional email) documented in our Privacy Policy.
