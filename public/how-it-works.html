How JustFit Works

Version 1.0 — Effective 1 April 2025

Scope

An overview of how JustFit generates personalised daily workout plans, coaches you through sessions, and tracks your progress over time.

Daily Calibration (Optional)

Each day, you can tell JustFit how you're doing: sleep, energy, mood, stress, and any practical constraints (no gym, travelling, short on time, pain). This takes under 30 seconds.

If you skip the check-in or set it to manual, JustFit still generates a plan based on your goal, experience level, equipment, and schedule — automatically, without any input needed.

The Planner Engine

A rule-based engine (not AI) generates a personalised session. Rules fire in priority order — life always wins over training preferences:

  • Pain reported → rest day, no exceptions
  • Low sleep (≤5h) → intensity and volume reduced
  • High stress → session shifts toward mobility
  • Travelling or no gear → bodyweight-only session
  • No time (≤10 min) → micro session, 2 exercises
  • Pregnancy → high-impact excluded, pelvic floor included
  • Postnatal → phase-gated recovery programme
  • Running Coach enrolled → structured run session prescribed
  • Outdoor exercises always scheduled last

Goals & Experience

Set your training goal (general health, lose weight, build strength, build muscle, endurance, or mobility & flex) and experience level (beginner to advanced). These drive exercise count, sets, rest periods, intensity, and session naming.

Cycle & Body Awareness

Female users can enable Smart Cycle Tracking. The planner adjusts intensity and volume across the four cycle phases. Pregnancy and postnatal modes activate a full set of safety rules — filtering high-impact exercises, adding pelvic floor work, and respecting medical clearance stages.

Workout Coaching

During a session, JustFit coaches you step by step: instruction cards, a rep tap zone, automatic rest timer, difficulty controls (+/− reps or seconds), and exercise alternatives. The screen stays on via Wake Lock so you never need to touch your phone mid-set.

Consistency Score

Your score (0–100) is calculated from the last 7 days:

  • Active days × 10 (max 70)
  • Resilience bonus for easy-day sessions × 5 (max 20)
  • Continuity streak bonus (max 10)

It rewards showing up — especially on hard days.

History & Awards

Every completed session is logged. The History tab shows your recent workouts with duration and effort rating. The Awards tab tracks 26 milestone achievements — from your first session to 100-day streaks.
